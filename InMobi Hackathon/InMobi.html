Smoke Scream

This is an android app which attempts to aid people in the process of quitting smoking. In this process, there are times when he might get the urge to pick up a cigarette again. If he tries and resists, and he's still early on in the process,the telltale signs are that his hands will start trembling, he tends to type wrongly on the keypad (including repetitive correction of his words) and he might start sweating as well .Accounting for these parameters using the sensors on the phone,we trigger a message-send to a friend who can probably call him and talk him through the urge, as well as a motivational message from the admin.
